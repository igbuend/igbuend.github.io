March 17, 2021 - 1 minutes read - 121 words

Welcome to pool.malpertus.com!

This is a new project for us. We just deployed our first Malpertus Cardano Staking node.

It is a small node, but we are in it for the long run, since we want to support the Cardano project and give it the effort it needs.

Since we are based in Singapore, we will do our best to expand the Cardano network in South East Asia. Currently our node is in Singapore, with two public relay servers in Singapore, and one private relay server in Canada. We plan to have a relay node in Malaysia live early April 2021.

Have fun exploring this site, hope you have a few laughs, but also learn a few things.
